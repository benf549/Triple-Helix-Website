2019-2020 Events

Intelligence and the Art of Science Communication with Dr. Aliza Watters

A collaboration with Common Question, a new undergraduate program that seeks to create interdisciplinary engagement between students and faculty around a fundamental question. This year's question is "What is Intelligence?" We were extremely fortunate to have Dr. Aliza Watters lead a discussion on this topic. Dr. Aliza Watters is the co-founder of Common Question and is a recipient of a JH Faculty Undergraduate Teaching Award. Her work focuses on how to blend science and the humanities to better communicate research and scholarship, it featured a fun discussion about science, writing, and intelligence.

American Society for Cell Biology/European Molecular Biology Organization Conference

An exciting opportunity to send members to the American Society for Cell Biology's annual conference in Washington, DC! This is an international conference hosted in partnership with the European Molecular Biology Organization. Members attended to both present their research and learn about the current happenings in the field of cell biology.
