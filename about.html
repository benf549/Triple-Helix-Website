WHO WE ARE

We are a completely student-run organization that publishes an annual journal called The Science in Society Review. This publication aims to act as a platform for students to discuss topics they are passionate about and foster a deeper understanding of the societal impact of an ever-changing scientific field. We work to promote active discussion of scientific developments through events. We are also working to implement a new E-Publication blog to give students the opportunity to be published more frequently. In addition to our publications, we also work to host events on campus dedicated to bringing attention to how scientific advancements may stand to impact us in the future as well as attend conferences that align with the academic interests of our members.
